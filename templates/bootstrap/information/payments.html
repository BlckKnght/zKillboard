zKillboard - Payments

zKillboard
  • You can make payments to zKillboard that will remove ads when you are logged in.
  • You can be ad free on zKillboard.com and all subdomains when you are logged into your account.
  • Each month of ad free time costs {{ adFreeMonthCost|number_format(2) }} ISK.
  • Each 6 months you purchase will give you a bonus month.
  • To make a payment send ISK to the character zKillboard, or use this handy in game link: zKillboard.
  • The wallet is checked approximately every 35 minutes, the next wallet check is at {{ info.NextWalletFetch }}.

Account

Two methods of payment, pick one!
  • Include your account name in the reason code. E.g., if your login is squizz, then include squizz in the payment reason. Must be an exact match!
  • Have an API tied to your account that includes the character you sent the ISK from.

Subdomain

  • To make a payment for a subdomain, all you have to do is include the URL, and only the URL of the subdomain in the payment reason.
